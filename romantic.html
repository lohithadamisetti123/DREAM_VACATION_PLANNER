Romantic Getaway Destinations

1. Santorini, Greece

Famous for its whitewashed buildings and sunsets, Santorini is a dreamy escape for couples seeking charm and sea views.

Why Visit: Caldera views, cliffside dining, boutique stays from ₹4000/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

2. Udaipur, India

Known as the “City of Lakes,” Udaipur offers palaces, boat rides, and royal ambiance perfect for a romantic Indian retreat.

Why Visit: Lake Pichola, City Palace, heritage stays from ₹1500/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

3. Venice, Italy

Venice enchants lovers with gondola rides, charming canals, and Italian romance around every corner.

Why Visit: Gondolas, St. Mark’s Square, stays from ₹3500/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

4. Paris, France

The “City of Love” lives up to its name with candle-lit dinners, Eiffel Tower views, and timeless romance.

Why Visit: Seine cruises, Louvre Museum, romantic hotels from ₹4000/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

5. Sapa, Vietnam

Nestled in the hills of Vietnam, Sapa is ideal for couples seeking nature, peace, and foggy, cozy vibes.

Why Visit: Terraced rice fields, homestays, quiet evenings from ₹1000–₹2000/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

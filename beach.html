Beach Destinations

1. Bali, Indonesia

Bali, the “Island of the Gods,” blends spirituality with surf. From stunning temples to volcanic mountains, it offers a culturally rich escape on a budget.

Why Visit: Surf Kuta, explore Tanah Lot, and find stays from ₹830–₹1660 per night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

2. Palawan, Philippines

Turquoise lagoons and limestone cliffs define Palawan. It's perfect for snorkeling, kayaking, and nature lovers seeking peaceful retreats.

Why Visit: Discover El Nido, Coron, and enjoy stays from ₹1245–₹2070 per night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

3. Varkala, India

Varkala’s cliffside beach views and laid-back vibe make it a soulful destination for yoga, wellness, and spiritual travelers.

Why Visit: Yoga retreats, clifftop cafes, budget stays from ₹500 per night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

4. Zanzibar, Tanzania

This spice island offers exotic beaches and a mix of Arab, Persian, and African heritage. A paradise for history buffs and beach lovers alike.

Why Visit: Roam Stone Town, relax at Nungwi Beach, stays around ₹1660/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

5. Koh Tao, Thailand

This tiny Thai island is a diver’s dream with rich marine life and budget-friendly bungalows, making it ideal for backpackers.

Why Visit: Learn scuba diving, vibrant nightlife, stays from ₹830–₹1245 per night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

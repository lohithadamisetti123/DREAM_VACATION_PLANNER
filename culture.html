Culture & Heritage Destinations

1. Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto is a timeless city, blending centuries-old temples, tea ceremonies, and traditional wooden houses. It reflects Japan’s soul.

Why Visit: Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, stroll Gion, cultural stays from ₹3000/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

2. Varanasi, India

Varanasi, one of the world’s oldest cities, is rich in Hindu traditions, Ganga Aarti, and sacred ghats — a spiritual and cultural journey.

Why Visit: Attend Ganga Aarti, explore Kashi Vishwanath, budget stays from ₹600–₹1200/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

3. Istanbul, Turkey

Istanbul merges East and West with mosques, palaces, and bustling bazaars — a cultural melting pot of two continents.

Why Visit: Visit Hagia Sophia, shop Grand Bazaar, stays from ₹2000/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

4. Rome, Italy

Rome, the Eternal City, is a living museum with ruins, churches, and art spanning thousands of years of human civilization.

Why Visit: Walk the Colosseum, visit Vatican City, hostels from ₹2500/night.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

5. Petra, Jordan

Petra is an archaeological wonder carved into red rock cliffs. This UNESCO site takes you back to the Nabataean civilization.

Why Visit: Explore the Treasury, hike through the Siq, budget-friendly lodges from ₹1500/night.

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)
